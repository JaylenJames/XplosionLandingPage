Where Injury Prevention For Greater Performance Becomes Reality

Xplosion is developing sensor technology to track and improve the performance of athletes.

Overview

A Personal Coaching Assistant, Xplosion is a 4D Motion Baseball Training system that is Providing a range of sensors that you can put on the Body and Sporting Equipment

Body Awareness

Players get to see and adjust to weak areas in both body movements and equipment

Swing Efficiency

Player can compare swing sessions and analyze productivity

Training Solutions

Our unique algorithms will provide training tips on how to improve on weak areas.

Mobile

Fully integrated for Web & Mobile, providing all the data you need at your fingertips.

How it works

First, attach the body and swing sensor. When double tapped, the recording of data begins and is streamed on the mobile apps. A swing is analyzed when completed and recommendations & improvements are offered
